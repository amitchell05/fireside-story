It was a dark and stormy night...

All of the kittens were sleeping quietly...

When all of the sudden, they are awaken by the sounds of deep howls...

The kittens shivered and were shocked to see a bright flash of fire!

Ten dogs are fleeing the military base a few yards away.

At the sound of the barking, the kittens dash out onto the porch, and the eldest looks out into the darkness.

It sees it's mother jumping out of the highest tree, and flies with a rainbow trail containing powerful magic!

The kittens squeal with delight and jump on the rainbow trail as their mother zooms past, whisking them off to safety. Then they hear thunder in the distance.
